Getting Started

Here is the best way to learn this system:

  1. Close out of all other programs except your browser.
  2. Follow the instructions on the left for "Set up and Sign In" the first time (only).

  3. Click "Scheduling" to open up the MindBodyOnline Scheduler


    If you are using Internet Explorer it will open up in a new window and you will need to use the instructions below.

    ****If you are using Firefox then click HERE
    and follow these one-time setup instructions instead.****

    I highly recommend using Firefox instead.  It will import all of your bookmarks from ie and is a much better browser.  It acts just like ie, but there are less pop-ups, less worries about security, less virus vulnerability and you have the nice feature of tabbed browsing (neat little tabs at the top where you can go from screen to screen).  Best of all, it's completely free.  You can download the current version HERE.

    Internet Explorer Users

  4. Hold down the ctrl button and click tab which should bring you back to here.  Use cntrl-tab to go back and forth easily between Intranet and MindBodyOnline.  (as long as nothing else is open on your computer's desktop)
  5. Then under "Table of Contents" click on your job (ex: PC).


     
  6. Then the menu will move down to the specific tasks that you may want to do under your category.  Just click on what you want and it will open the appropriate instructions in the right hand frame.  You can scroll up or down either frame at will and click "back to top" if you need to.
  7. Click ctrl-tab to bring you over to the scheduler and follow the instructions.  ctrl-tab back and forth and you can do it one step at a time.
