How to Practice the AHC Computer System
  1. Create a new record:
    Go to ahc.webexone.com -> all_clients database and create a new client named Testy Testerson. 
    • Then fill in all the fields just like he's a real lead. 
    • Make sure you fill in all the required fields. 
    • Under Sales Stage - choose A1-call immediately. 
    • Under record manager - choose your name.
    • Then click "Save" at the top of the screen.
       
  2. Find that person as if it's a real live lead that Meredith had entered:
    Go to http://ahc.webexone.com -> all_clients database
    • The view at the top should say "A1-Leads by Sales Stage" and the first category is "1 - Call immediately."  Scroll down and find Testy Testerson. 
    • If he's not there, perhaps you need to look at more files.  By default, the database only shows the first 25.  To see more records scroll up to the top and look above the dark blue bar and find where it says: 
      5059 items. Showing 1-25. Show All  Next
      and click next to show the next screen or All so show every record (which would be a LOT).
    • When you find Testy Testerson, click on the pencil next to his name to open his record for editing. 
  3. Look up nearest therapist (if not in Richmond):
    • Open a new window or a new tab if you need to.
    • Open up www.americanhypnosisclinic.com/intranet and click on Affiliates.
    • Then enter the client's zip code into the Find The Closest Therapist blue box and click "Submit."
    • That will give you a list of the nearest therapists.  You can click on the one nearest the client and read a little about this person.
  4. Bring up the Richmond therapist's calendars (if lead is in Richmond)
    • Open a new window or a new tab if you need to.
    • www.americanhypnosisclinic.com/intranet and on the left frame scroll down until you see "Calendar."
    • Click on the little green downward-pointing-arrow inside the calendar button and from that drop-down menu click "this month."
    • When that monthly calendar comes up click on "View calendar for" and change from "me" to Dan Lester, Scott Moody or Heather Merrill to choose one of the Richmond therapists.  (Scott and Dan should always be scheduled first and then Heather.)
    • You may want to open one tab or window for each therapist so you can click back and forth most quickly.
  5. Bring up the Affiliate's Calendar (if lead is NOT in Richmond)
    • When you look in the affiliate's information (www.americanhypnosisclinic.com/intranet) see if they have an online calendar.  If it's yahoo then go to http://calendar.yahoo.com and then enter his username and password (listed in intranet) and you can check his calendar from there.  If it's MSN then go to www.hotmail.com and access his calendar from there.
    • Most of the time it will say "call me" or put three weeks out.  If the client is on the phone then just pick an arbitrary date three weeks out and schedule the person then.  Immediately contact the therapist and make sure that they can make that date or change it with the client if needed.  If the client is not on the phone, then call the therapist first and get a range of open dates to choose from.  Then call Testy Testerson (imagine) and work out an appointment date. 
    • If you cannot get hold of the affiliate before calling the client, call the client anyway and set an arbitrary date for 3 weeks out.  It is easy to re-schedule an appointment for a client and most won't be concerned about it.  Whereas you can lose sales if you cannot get back to the person or pin down a date because of difficulties getting in touch with a therapist.
